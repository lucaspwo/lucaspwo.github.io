## About me

{% p %}Currently, I'm a developer at Wla SaaS, working with React in the next portal of services management of the company. Previously, I worked with embedded systems such as Toradex and Raspberry Pi, besides developing with React, Django, Node, Python and Microsoft SQL Server.{% endp %}

{% p %}I graduated in Computer Engineering at Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Norte (Brazil) and I also have a Bachelor's degree in Science and Technology, graduated in the same institution. I always had interest for anything that light an LED or shows sensor data with the press of a button, might it be real or virtual.{% endp %}

{% p %}In these last years, I developed projects for the Internet of Things for remote monitoring or for small automations, and created mobile apps utilizing the React Native framework.
